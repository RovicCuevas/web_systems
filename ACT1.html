Introduction

Hello, my name is Eren Yeager, but you can just call me Eren.

I'm from Shiganshina District and I’m 19 years old. My birthday is March 30, 835, and I was a former recruit

of the 104th Training Corps, later becoming a member of the Survey Corps.

My mother’s name was Carla Yeager, and my father’s name was Grisha Yeager. I also have a foster

sister named Mikasa Ackerman and a best friend named Armin Arlert. My hobbies include training,

fighting Titans, and striving for freedom. Most of the time, I spend thinking about the outside

world and humanity’s future. My dream is to see the world beyond the walls.

“The only thing we're allowed to do is believe that we won't regret the choice we made.” – Levi Ackerman
